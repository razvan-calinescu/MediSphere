Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

The answers to the questions our customers commonly ask

help_outline Do I need an appointment to use MediSphere services?

Yes, an appointment is required for using our services. Please proceed to scheduling an appointment online, by phone or in person at our front desk.

 help_outline How long does it take to receive test results?

Basic test results will be available online, in MyMediSphere platform, on the sampling day, after 14:00. However, some more complex tests can take longer to be finished. In this case you'll be announced by our staff.

 help_outline Are you a partner of CNAS for price deduction?

Yes. We collaborate with CNAS and any procedure done in our hospital is subject to price deduction, depending on the available sum

 help_outline Do you provide emergency services?

Yes. Our hospital provides emergency services, disposing of a 24/7 Trauma Center and a fleet of new generation fully-equipped ambulances
